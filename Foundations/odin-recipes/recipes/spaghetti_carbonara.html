Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's creamy, delicious, and comes together in just a few minutes, making it perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Spaghetti Carbonara Ingredients

  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 ounces pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese. Set aside.
  3. In a large pan, cook pancetta or bacon over medium heat until crispy. Add minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds.
  4. Reduce heat to low and add the drained spaghetti to the pan, tossing to coat with the pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg and cheese mixture, tossing continuously to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water a little at a time if needed.
  6. Season with black pepper and salt to taste.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley, if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy!
